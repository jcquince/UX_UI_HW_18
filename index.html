Hey, I'm Jason Quince!

I’m a user experience designer that focuses on crafting accessible and intuitive design experiences. I’m passionate about using empathy and design to solve problems in this quickly evolving tech world in which we live.

My UX Skills

User Research and Testing

Working with users is arguably the most important aspect of UX design, and knowing what questions to ask and how to interpret the answers to provide the right product is crucial.

Collaboration

Collaborating with others is at the center of UX design, whether that be talking with users, designers, developers, or clients. Being able to work with others in a friendly and effiecient manner will make designing a great product that much easier.

Prototyping & Iteration

Taking user feedback and creating products that solve their problems is what makes good design. Being able to then iterate upon that design based on further feedback and continue to refine the product is what makes a UX designer valuable to a team.
